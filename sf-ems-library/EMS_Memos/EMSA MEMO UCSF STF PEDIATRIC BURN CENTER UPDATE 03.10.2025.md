Subject: UPDATE (3/7/2025) UCSF Saint Francis Pediatric Burn Center Receiving Center Status

*****UPDATE*****

This is an update to a previously issued memo dated November 4, 2024 referencing UCSF Health Saint Francis
Memorial Hospitalâ€™s ability to no longer accept any pediatric burn patients.

Effective Monday, March 10 at 0700, all critical pediatric burn patients as defined under Policy 5000 that do
not meet trauma triage criteria should be transported from the field to UCSF Saint Francis as has previously
been long standing practice.

Critical pediatric burn patients with trauma triage criteria should continue to be transported to Zuckerberg
San Francisco General (ZSFG) as has also been long standing practice.

Policy 5000 (Destination Policy) and 5000.1 (San Francisco Hospital Designations) are in the process of being
updated to reflect this change and will be available on sf.gov/EMSA and within the protocol app shortly.

The EMS Agency appreciates all system partners in messaging updates to staff regarding the changes in
pediatric burn center destinations. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact
the EMS Agency.
