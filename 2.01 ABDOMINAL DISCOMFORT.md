2.01 ABDOMINAL DISCOMFORT

BLS Treatment

•  Position of comfort.
•  NPO
•  Oxygen as indicated.

ALS Treatment

IV / IO of Normal Saline TKO.
If SBP <90 or signs of poor perfusion, Normal Saline fluid bolus.

•
•
•  For pain refer to Protocol 2.09 Pain Control.
•  For nausea/vomiting may administer Ondansetron.
