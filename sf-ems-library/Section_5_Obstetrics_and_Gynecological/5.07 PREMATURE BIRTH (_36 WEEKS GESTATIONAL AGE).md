BLS Treatment

•

•  Assess circulation, airway, breathing, and responsiveness of mother and fetus.
•

If greater than 20 weeks gestational age:  Attempt to resuscitate and transport to Pediatric
Critical Care Center. See Protocol 8.05 Newborn/Neonatal Resuscitation.
If less than or equal to 20 weeks gestational age:  Wrap baby in blanket. Allow family to
hold baby if desired and offer emotional/grief support as appropriate. Place all other uterine
contents that are expelled during delivery in a biohazard bag and bring to Receiving Hospital.
If heavy vaginal bleeding, firmly rub abdomen below navel with flat hand x 15 seconds
