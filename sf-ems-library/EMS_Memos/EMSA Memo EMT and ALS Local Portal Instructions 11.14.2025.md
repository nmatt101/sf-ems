INSTRUCTIONS for Paramedics:

Step 1: Visit the EMS Agency certifications portal by visiting:
https://sanfrancisco.imagetrendlicense.com/lms/public/portal#/login

Step 2: All ALS Local accredited paramedics have an existing account as part of the transition to a
paperless platform. Click on “Forgot Username” and follow the prompts to claim your account. You will
be prompted to provide an email. Please use your work email if you are issued one. Your last name must
match exactly with the name on file with the EMS Agency to claim your account (check your most recent
accreditation card). You will receive an email with your username with a link to reset your password.
Check your spam folder if you do not receive the email. If you are unable to access your account, please
email emsacertifications@sfgov.org. If you previously applied for and received a Community
Paramedicine or Triage to Alternate Destination renewal, you do not need to claim your account and
can just proceed to log-in to your account. Do not create a duplicate account as it will be deleted and
result in delayed review of your application.

Step 3: Log-in and navigate to the menu on left-hand side of page. Select “Applications”

Step 4: Select “View All Personnel Applications”

Step 5: Select the ALS Local application by clicking “Apply Now” button.

Step 6: Employment and training verification will be automatically routed to your employer on file. If
your employer is incorrectly listed on your application, please email emsacertifications@sfgov.org
before submitting your application.

Step 7: Monitor your email closely. Once you submit an application, you will receive a confirmation
email. Once your application is approved, you will receive an email with your Local ALS Accreditation
e-card as an attachment. Physical cards are no longer issued for paramedic accreditation.
