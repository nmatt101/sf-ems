3.03 (NEAR) DROWNING

BLS Treatment

•  Position of comfort.
•  NPO
•  Oxygen as indicated.

ALS Treatment

•  Advanced airway intervention, as necessary.
•  Cardiac monitor.
•

IV or IO of Normal Saline TKO.

Comments

•  Rapid transport. Patient can deteriorate rapidly.
•  Description (salt or fresh water) and temperature of submersion fluid.
•  Duration of submersion.
•  Height of fall/mechanism of injury.
•  Suspicion of alcohol or other drugs/medications involved.
•  Evidence of head/ spinal trauma or other associated injuries.
•  Neurologic status.
•  Respiratory findings: rales or signs of pulmonary edema, respiratory distress.

Base Hospital Contact Criteria

•  To withhold or cease resuscitation due to witnessed submersion of > 25 minutes or no

response to treatment.
