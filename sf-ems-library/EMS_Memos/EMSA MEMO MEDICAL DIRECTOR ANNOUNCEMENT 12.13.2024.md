Subject: Welcome Dr. Amelia Breyre - San Francisco EMS Agency Medical Director

Please join me in welcoming Dr. Amelia Breyre as the San Francisco EMS Agency Medical Director!

Dr. Breyre is a board-certified Emergency Medicine (EM) and EMS physician. She completed medical
school at the University of Pennsylvania, EM residency at Highland Hospital and EMS/Disaster
Fellowship at UCSF/ZSFGH. She has worked clinically and administratively throughout different hospital
systems in the Bay Area and California. Most recently she has served as Associate EMS Medical Director
for 27 EMS Agencies in the Yale New Haven Health System and Assistant Professor at the Yale
Emergency Department School of Medicine in Connecticut. She is eager to be joining the San Francisco
EMS Agency and looking forward to collaborating with one of the most innovative EMS systems in the
country.

Dr. Breyre is planning to assume the role in mid-March 2025. A special thank you to Dr. Tenner for her
leadership during this transition and assuming the role of Interim Medical Director for the past year.

For any questions, please contact the EMS Agency.
