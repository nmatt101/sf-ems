Subject: Interim San Francisco EMS Agency Medical Director

Please join me in welcoming Dr. Andi Tenner as the interim San Francisco EMS Agency Medical Director.
To maintain continuity of medical direction oversight, Dr. Tenner will be assuming the role starting
March 7. Dr. Tenner currently serves as the Director of the Public Health Emergency Preparedness and
Response branch under the Department of Public Health. Dr. Tenner is an emergency physician who
works locally at Zuckerburg San Francisco General and passionate about disaster preparedness and
global health.

Thank you to Dr. Brown and Dr. Tenner for their service, guidance, and leadership during this transition.
The recruitment process for a full-time, permanent medical director is underway and will share more
information regarding the job posting in the coming weeks.

For any questions, please contact the EMS Agency.
