Subject: Policy 5000 - Pediatric Behavioral Health Patients

As a reminder, EMS Agency Policy 5000 – Destination Policy states that non-critical pediatric patients, including
behavioral health/psychiatric, may go to any Receiving Facility within San Francisco. If the patient has a medical
home other than UCSF Mission Bay or CPMC - Van Ness (Critical Pediatric Specialty Centers), the pediatric patient
can be transported to any Receiving Facility as long as no other specialty criteria is met.

For any questions or concerns, please contact the EMS Agency.
